Eggs + Olive Oil Did you know that you could make a super conditioning pack with raw eggs and some olive oil? Just beat together 2-3 eggs with 5-6 tablespoons of olive oil and then apply the mixture on to dry hair. For its maximum effect, cover the hair with a plastic shower cap, or wrap in a plastic wrap. The body heat that is produced in the plastic cover will further help in opening up the pores in the hair shaft to allow the conditioner to reach deep into the hair follicles. View Photos Rosemary + Apple Cider Vinegar + Water This lovely mixture is a homemade hair care nurturer that produces shine and volume and is made from an infusion of fresh rosemary, apple cider vinegar and water. Add a few springs of rosemary to a cup of a mixture of apple cider vinegar and water. Leave it in a sealed container for a few days, and you'll have a wonderful rinse to use after shampooing the tresses that'll leave the hair shiny and refreshed.
